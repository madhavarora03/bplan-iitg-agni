Delhi Fire Services

We are also recognized by the Delhi Fire Services (DFS), a government agency responsible for rescuing the city and its residents from the dangers of uncontrolled fires. The DFS has become our primary supplier of decommissioned fire hoses.

Ministry of Transport

With the collaboration of the Ministry of Transport, we are able to provide livelihoods to E-Rickshaw drivers who are employed to transport raw materials from the DFS to Najafgarh.

Tihar Emporio

Empowering incarcerated individuals by providing them with vocational skills in manufacturing Agni products can offer them a meaningful and productive path forward. The sale of these products not only provides them with a sense of purpose but also a means to support themselves.

Govt of Delhi

Project Agni is actively partnering with the Delhi government to establish SHGs (self-help groups) for women across various regions in Delhi. Presently, we have more than 120 women participating in 12+ SHGs, and this number continues to grow.
